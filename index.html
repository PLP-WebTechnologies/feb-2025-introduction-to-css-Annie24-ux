📍 Why I Love Travel

Travel opens my mind, helps me meet new people, and lets me experience cultures beyond my own. Exploring the world is my biggest dream, and I’m excited to someday turn it into reality.

🌍 Top Destinations I Want to Visit

🇰🇪 Kenya

To experience the Great Migration, explore Nairobi, relax on the Swahili coast, and feel deeply connected to my African heritage.

🇯🇵 Japan

From Tokyo’s vibrant city life to the peaceful temples of Kyoto, Japan blends technology and tradition in a way that fascinates me.

🇮🇹 Italy

Italy’s food, history, and art are legendary. I dream of seeing the Colosseum, eating pasta in Rome, and exploring the canals of Venice.

🇲🇦 Morocco

Colorful markets, desert adventures, and ancient cities — Morocco promises a magical experience.
